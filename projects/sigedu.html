University Platform (SIGEDU)

SIGEDU is a modular digital platform for academic and administrative management in universities and technical institutes. It supports student enrollment, course scheduling, evaluation tracking, financial control, and reporting—all within a centralized system.

  • Technologies: Web-based platform, PHP, MySQL, JavaScript, REST APIs

  • Web Site: SIGEDU

  • SIGEDU integrates academic processes (such as subject planning, student records, exams, and grades) with institutional back-office operations (like tuition billing and reporting). Its modular design allows universities to adopt only the components they need. The system is fully web-based and optimized for public and private institutions across Latin America.
